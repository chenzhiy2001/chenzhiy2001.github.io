PARTICIPATE (Phase 2)

INPUTS

Sources of information:

  • Existing knowledge on the topic (the visual map you prepared in Phase 1).
  • Lecture notes (A3.3).

THE 4 REPEATING STEPS

Identify key concepts:

  • What are the new key concepts that you need to add to your visual map?
  • Capture important teacher cues.

Organize and connect key concepts in your visual map in a meaningful way:

  • How do the new concepts fit with your existing concepts?
  • Is there a better way to arrange the key concepts in your visual map?

Think critically:

  • Are there any patterns?
  • Does this make sense?
  • Where is the emphasis?

Ask key questions:

  • How does one state of matter change to another state of matter? Why?
  • How can one separate different states of matter?
