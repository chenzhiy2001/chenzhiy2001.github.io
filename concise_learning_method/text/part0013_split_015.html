INPUTS

Sources of information:

THE 4 REPEATING STEPS

Identify key concepts:

  • What key concepts is this problem testing?

Organize and connect key concepts in your visual map in a meaningful way:

  • You should update and revise your visual map as soon as possible after the exam.

Think critically:

  • Do you understand the problem?
  • Have you solved a similar problem in the past?
  • What are the steps needed to solve this problem?

Ask key questions:

  • What key concept is this problem testing?
  • How can you solve the problem with what you already know?
